About

The overall idea and concept for the Smoke Keychain browser extension was created by @yabapmatt who is a developer and witness on the SMOKE blockchain. It was primarily developed by @stoodkev who is also a SMOKE developer (SMOKEPlus) and witness. The design and development were funded by @aggroed (a top 20 SMOKE witness) and @yabapmatt through their joint business SMOKE Monsters, which is a blockchain-based collectible trading card game built on top of the SMOKE blockchain. Be sure to check it out at SMOKEmonsters.com!
